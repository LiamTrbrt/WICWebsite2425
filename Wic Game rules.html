In a 2v2 Game, two teams, blue and yellow, have to battle it out to get the most amount of points possible, in a respected time rperiod of 5 minutes! The playingfield, is one giant tic tac toe board, with stations in each square. The goal: capture the stations to get three in a row, and than those stations are worth 50% more. To capture a station, you must grap a ring with your robot, and put it on the stations pole. and at the end of the time, the different stations are won by whoever has the most rings on that station, and all the points are tallied up, and a winning team is chosen based on the amount of points.
